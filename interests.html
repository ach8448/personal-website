Interests

I was introduced to programming in my junior year of high school. Coding was fun, but it was when I got to college and exposed myself to more of the Computer Science field that I developed a fascination for the opportunities and solutions that are possible. Data analytics and Product Management are fields that I intend to pursue, and I look forward to honing my skills in these industries!

Artificial Intelligence

As Web 3.0 is increasingly present in our lives, it is important to establish ethical practices sooner rather than later. I hope to explore more of what AI has to offer and solving complex, real-world problems.

Sustainability

Many researchers have high expectations regarding AI being the solution to many of our problems. However, many are talking about full automation. There is limited work done that prioritizes including human participation, which I believe is where the most potential lies. If we don't aim to change habits, automation is only a short-term solution.

Animals

I am an avid animal lover, and I have two cats and one fish! During my free time, you can often find me at a local cat cafe. Animals play a large role in so many lives, and I would love to support animal systems.

Violin

I have been playing the violin for 14 years. I participated in the Principal Orchestra of the Metropolitan Youth Orchestra(MYO) throughout high school. With MYO, I have performed at renowned venues such as Carnegie Hall, Staller Center, and Tilles Center.
